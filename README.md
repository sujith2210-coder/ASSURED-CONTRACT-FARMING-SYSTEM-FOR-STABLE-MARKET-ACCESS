# 🌾 Assured Contract Farming System for Stable Market Access

## 📌 Overview

The **Assured Contract Farming System for Stable Market Access** is a web-based platform that connects farmers and buyers through secure digital contracts. It enables farmers to receive guaranteed market access before cultivation while allowing buyers to procure quality produce directly from trusted farmers. The system promotes transparency, fair pricing, and efficient agricultural trade by reducing the dependency on intermediaries.

---

## ✨ Features

* 👨‍🌾 Farmer Registration & Login
* 🛒 Buyer Registration & Login
* 🔐 Secure User Authentication
* 📄 Digital Contract Management
* 🌱 Crop Listing & Management
* 💰 Transparent Pricing
* 📦 Order & Contract Tracking
* 🔔 Notifications & Updates
* 👨‍💼 Admin Dashboard
* 📊 User & Contract Monitoring
